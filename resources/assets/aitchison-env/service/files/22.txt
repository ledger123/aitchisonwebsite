Gulmohar

Delonix regia is a species of flowering plants in the bean family Fabaceae, subfamily Caesalpinioideae native to Madagascar. It is noted for its fern-like leaves and flamboyant display of orange-red flowers over summer.

Gulmohar has divergent medicinal properties like antibacterial, antifungal, anti-inflammatory, antimalarial, antimicrobial, antioxidant, cardio-protective, gastro-protective, and wound healing activity. Leaves have anti-diabetic properties, and using its methanol extract significantly lowers blood glucose levels.

Gulmohar is an ornament plant also known as a flame tree. Gulmohar is well known for its beautiful flowers.
