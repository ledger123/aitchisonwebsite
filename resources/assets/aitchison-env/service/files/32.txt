Dhokra

Anogeissus latifolia (Dhokra) is a crucial multipurpose tree, not fast-growing, which produces hardwood timber utilized for a wide range of construction purposes, and which is also a good source of fuelwood and charcoal. It also produces gum, 'Ghatti Gum', used as a substitute for gum Arabic.

Anogeissus latifolia is one of the essential medicinal plants in cardiac disorders. The plant is helpful in UTI infections, skin diseases, liver complaints, fever, epileptic fits, etc. The plant is rich in pharmacologically active phenolic phytoconstituent-ellagic acid.
