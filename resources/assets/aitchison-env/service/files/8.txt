Plikhan Tree

Plikhan Tree (Ficus virens) also known as the White Fig Tree is a plant of the Genus Ficus found in Southeast Asia, through Malaysia and into Northern Australia. It is a part of the Moraceae tree family.It is a medium-sized tree which grows to a height of 24–27 metres (79–89 ft), in dry areas and up to 32 metres (105 ft) tall in wetter areas.

One of the most famous specimens of this tree is the Curtain Fig Tree of the Atherton Tableland, near Cairns, a popular tourist attraction.It is a popular medicinal plant in India, which has long been used in Ayurveda, the ancient system of Indian medicine, for various diseases/disorders.
