MANGO TREES

Mango trees, (Mangifera indica), is a species of flowering plant in the family Anacardiaceae. It is a large fruit tree, capable of growing to a height of 30-100 Metres.

Mango trees can grow quite large, reaching heights of 30 to 100 feet (9 to 30 meters). They have a dense, rounded canopy with shiny, dark green leaves, which can measure up to 16 inches (40 cm) in length. It is one of the most popular and widely cultivated fruit trees in the world. Approximately 500 varieties of this tree have been reported in South East Asia.
