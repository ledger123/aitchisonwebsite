Beri
Beri trees may be erect or wide-spreading, with gracefully drooping thorny branches. The leaves are alternate, ovate or oblong elliptic, with 3 veins at the base, and are usually about 2.5 to 3.2 cm long and 1.8 to 3.8 cm wide. The flowers are yellow, 5-petalled and are usually in twos and threes in the leaf axils.
It is used for skin rejuvenation, wound healing, promoting digestion, strengthening bones, and weight loss. It has potential in the use of protecting the heart, anxiety, insomnia, memory, cancer, boost immunity and control blood sugar levels.
